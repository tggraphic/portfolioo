Project Overview

The Burger App is a web-based application designed to simplify the process of ordering and customizing delicious burgers online. It aims to provide a user-friendly and convenient experience for burger enthusiasts to create their perfect burger combinations and have them delivered to their doorstep.

Features

  • The "Build Your Own Burger" feature allows users to create their personalized burger by selecting from a wide range of options. Users can choose their preferred type of burger patty (e.g., beef, chicken, veggie), add various toppings (lettuce, tomato, onions, pickles, etc.), select their favorite cheese, and pick from an array of sauces. This feature empowers users to unleash their creativity and tailor the burger to suit their individual tastes and dietary preferences.
  • You can make an add to cart and order payment method
  • This project requires a backend to be more interactive
